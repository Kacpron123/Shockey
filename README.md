# Shockey
Air hockey with a high voltage twist

## How to play: 

Every time you hit the puck after your opponent, it transfers a charge to your paddle. If you accumulate too much energy, your paddle will overload, stunning you for 3 seconds. To prevent an overload, touch the top or bottom rails to safely discharge. Be careful though : both overloading and discharging leaves your goal wide open for a brief moment.

First to 7 points wins

## Controls

Player 1: WASD
Player 2: Arrow keys
Restart: R
